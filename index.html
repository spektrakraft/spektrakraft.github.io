Rules:

Every server should have rules. We need them to keep the server fun. Do not break these, if you do. There will be issues.

You cannot spam, no forms whatsoever.

Obey the staff. If they tell you not to do something, please do not do it.

Do not break other peoples things (EG: Houses, shops etc) without their permission. You can break land like you're playing regular Minecraft, just don't make it look ugly.

You cannot swear, no false swearing either (EG: Starring words), you can use other words like fudge and stuff.

You can voice your opinion. Blah blah blah, just be polite about it, please do not do it in a rude manner. There is freedom of speech.

No uncomfortable speech, that might make certain users scared or other.

Please report if anybody is breaking these rules: kraftspektra@gmail.com
